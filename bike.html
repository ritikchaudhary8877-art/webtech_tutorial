Cruiser Bike

Cruiser bikes are a type of motorcycle known for their classic, retro styling, often resembling American models from the 1930s-1960s.

They are designed primarily for a relaxed and comfortable riding experience, especially over long distances or for leisurely rides. Key features include a low seat height, wide, high handlebars, and forward-mounted foot pegs, which place the rider in an upright or slightly reclined posture, reducing back fatigue.

Their heavy frames and long wheelbases provide stability on the highway, and they are highly popular for customization, allowing riders to personalize their bikes with various accessories. Popular models include the Harley-Davidson Classic, Indian Scout, and Royal Enfield
