Commuter Bike

Commuter Bike is a vehicle designed for daily travel, such as to work, school, or for running errands, prioritizing comfort, reliability, and practicality over high performance.
Key features include a durable and low-maintenance design, an upright or comfortable riding position, and often integrated accessories like fenders and racks for carrying items and staying clean.

Features of Commuter Bikes:

  1. Comfort and stability
  2. Durability and low maintenance
  3. Practicality
    4. +
  4. Fuel efficiency
  5. Safety
